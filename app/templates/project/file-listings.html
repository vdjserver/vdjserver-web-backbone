Let's Get Started!

This program takes raw sequence data and generates an analysis report.

To begin this process, you will need to upload your raw sequencing data. You can also upload your primer sequences and barcode sequences here if you wish to identify and remove them from the raw read prior to alignment. If you upload a barcode file, VDJserver will separate and analyze your samples for you.

You can drag and drop to upload data or just click here.
